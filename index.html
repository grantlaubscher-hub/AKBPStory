Alex &
Bo Pig

When a mysterious war came through, Alex and her merle border collie walked out of their home in Banksia Beach and into an apocalyptic world.

The Beginning

Life was simple.

Sunrise walks on the beach. Salt in the air. Coffee on the deck. Bo Pig chasing birds and waves, always by Alex’s side.

They had a life they loved. It was ordinary in the way the best things are ordinary — unnoticed until the moment they are gone.

The Walk

Then came the war.

No warning. No reason. Just fire, explosions, and silence. The world outside the front door became strange, emptied, and broken.

Alex and Bo Pig did not have a plan. They just kept walking. One step. Then another. Together.

Front door Ash driveway Silent canal Burnt banksias Empty road The Passage
The World

The world is gone.

Cities are ruins. Nature is wild. The air is thick with dust and ash. Danger hides in every shadow.

But Alex and Bo Pig keep moving forward, following the coast, the wind, and the faint hope of another living soul.

The Bond

All they have is each other.

Bo Pig is not just a dog. He is Alex’s protector, her best friend, and her reason to keep going.

In a world that has fallen apart, their bond is unbreakable. When Alex cannot trust the road, she trusts Bo Pig.

The Hope

They keep walking.

Through the unknown. Toward a tomorrow they cannot see. Hope lives in every heartbeat, every pawstep, every sunrise.

As long as they are together, they will find a way.

They walk toward the light.

Alex with ash on her cheeks. Bo Pig with salt on his whiskers. Behind them, Banksia Beach waits in silence. Ahead, the broken world holds its breath.
