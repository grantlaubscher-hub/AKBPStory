A story of survival. A bond that remains.

When a mysterious war came through, Alex and her merle border collie, Bo Pig, walked out of their home in Banksia Beach and into a world changed beyond recognition. The roads were empty, the air was thick with dust and ash, and the silence felt larger than the sky.

They did not have a plan. They had each other. Bo Pig kept moving forward, nose low to the ground, ears raised toward every distant sound. Alex followed, step by step, through the ruins of a place that used to feel safe.

Somewhere beyond the smoke and broken streets, there was still light. As long as Alex and Bo Pig were together, there was still a way forward.
